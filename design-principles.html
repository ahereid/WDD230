Rule of Thirds

Paper Designs

The Paper Designs website uses the rule of thirds in its entirety. Clickable buttons that lead to the products are arranged closely to the four focal points.

White Space and Clean Design

Everlane

everlane.com

Everlane opts for a minimal set up: The full page background shows off a photograph of its new collection, and a small, expertly placed call-to-action appears in the center of the screen, encouraging users to click and "shop now." It's a perfect example of leading users towards an action without being pushy or aggressive.
